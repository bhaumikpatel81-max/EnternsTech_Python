FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before you start.

How are mentors selected?

Every mentor goes through a written application and interview process. We verify their technical background, communication skills, and availability. Approved mentors are matched to mentees based on tech stack overlap.

Can I change my mentor?

Yes. If the pairing isn't working, submit a change request from your dashboard with a brief reason. Admin reviews and reassigns you — no penalty.

What happens if I miss a session?

Sessions can be cancelled or rescheduled up to 24 hours in advance at no cost. Late cancellations are tracked. If your mentor is a no-show, you get a refund for that session automatically.

How does the CV redesign work?

Your assigned mentor reviews your current CV, rewrites it for ATS compatibility, and tailors it to the roles you're targeting. Progress is tracked in your dashboard.

Are payments secure?

Yes. Payments are processed by Razorpay. Your payment goes into escrow and is released to the mentor session-by-session as work is completed — protecting you throughout.

Is there a refund policy?

If a mentor cancels within SLA or is a no-show, the escrow for that session refunds automatically. Contact admin for other scenarios — we review within 48 hours.

Join hundreds of mentees who've landed tech roles with Enterns Tech. No subscriptions — just pay for the sessions you need.
